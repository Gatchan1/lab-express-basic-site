Main Works

Nana

2000-2009 (currently on hiatus)

"Nana" follows the lives of two young women, both named Nana, who meet on a train to Tokyo and form a deep bond. The story explores their journeys as they navigate the challenges of relationships, careers, and personal struggles, while supporting each other through life's ups and downs. With themes of love, friendship, and the pursuit of one's dreams, "Nana" is a captivating story of two women finding their way in the world.

Paradise Kiss

1999-2003

"Paradise Kiss" tells the story of Yukari, a high school student who is struggling to find meaning in her life. She meets a group of fashion design students who ask her to model for them and soon finds herself drawn into their world of creativity and self-expression. As Yukari becomes more involved with the group's leader, she begins to discover her own passion for fashion and a new sense of purpose in life.

Neighborhood Story

(Gokinjo monogatari)

1995-1997

"Neighborhood Story" is a story about a group of friends who attend an arts school in Tokyo. The main character, a fashion designer, navigates relationships and career aspirations while dealing with personal challenges. The story explores themes of self-discovery, creativity, and the pursuit of one's dreams.

I'm Not an Angel

(Tenshi nanka ja nai)

1991-1994

"I'm Not an Angel" is a series about a girl named Midori who is determined to become the student council president of her high school. Along the way, she meets a boy named Akira, who becomes her rival for the position. As they compete, they develop feelings for each other, and the story follows their journey of love, friendship, and personal growth.
